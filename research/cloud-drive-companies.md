## Notes & Methodology

1. **EU Status Classification:**
   - 游릭 European: HQ AND ultimate parent company in EU/EEA/Switzerland
   - 游리 Mixed: European presence but non-EU parent, or UK post-Brexit
   - 游댮 Non-European: HQ or ownership outside EU/EEA

2. **Switzerland Inclusion:** Switzerland is included as "European" due to:
   - Geographic proximity
   - GDPR adequacy decision
   - Swiss data protection law (DSG) alignment
   - Common perception as privacy-friendly European jurisdiction

3. **UK Post-Brexit:** UK companies classified as 游리 Mixed due to:
   - No longer EU member state
   - Different data protection regime possible
   - Ongoing regulatory divergence

4. **Data Sources:**
   - Wikipedia articles
   - Company websites
   - News articles and press releases
   - Company registration databases

5. **Last Updated:** January 2026
