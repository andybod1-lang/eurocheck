## Research Notes

### Key European Vertical Software Hubs

1. **Germany** - Largest European market with strong players in healthcare (CompuGroup Medical), construction (Nemetschek), fintech (Mambu), and HR tech (Personio)

2. **France** - Strong in banking software (Murex, Sopra Banking), logistics (Shippeo), and edtech (360Learning)

3. **Netherlands** - Growing fintech hub with Backbase, Mambu HQ, and strong proptech presence

4. **Nordics** - Strong in edtech (Kahoot!, itslearning) and accounting/ERP (Visma)

5. **Switzerland** - Banking software leader with Temenos, Avaloq, Finnova

### Ownership Complexity Notes

- Many "European" companies have significant PE ownership from US firms (Vista Equity, Thoma Bravo, TA Associates)
- Several US companies have EU parents (e.g., Bluebeam owned by Nemetschek)
- Acquisitions frequently change EU status (e.g., Transporeon acquired by Trimble)
