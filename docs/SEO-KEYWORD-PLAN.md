## Competitor Analysis

### Direct Competitors
1. **GoEuropean.eu** - Similar concept, simpler execution

   - Weaknesses: No browser extension, manual checking only

   - Strengths: Good SEO, content marketing
   - Weaknesses: No automation, passive experience
