Everything you need to know about EuroCheck

EuroCheck is a free browser extension that instantly shows whether online shops and websites are European-owned. It displays a small badge on sites, helping you easily identify and support European businesses while shopping online.

EuroCheck maintains a curated database of thousands of European e-commerce companies. When you visit a website, the extension checks if it matches our database and displays a visual indicator. The check happens locally and instantly ‚Äî no data is sent to external servers.

EuroCheck works on Chrome, Firefox, Edge, Brave, and other Chromium-based browsers. Both desktop and Android versions are supported where extensions are available.
